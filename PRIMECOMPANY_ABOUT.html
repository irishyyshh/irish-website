About The Prime Company

Curious about The Prime Company?

Read the information below and get a chance to know us better!

The Prime Company is a Filipino company based on Tagum City, Davao del Norte, Philippines.
It is owned by three (3) friends who have the same goals. It was established on the year 2024.
The Prime Company aims to create a welcoming and supportive community that prioritizes
empowerment, self-care, and inclusivity, where individuals can thrive and feel accepted.

FUN FACT!!

The Prime Company's name is compose of three names of a person.
"Pr" for Princess Dynah Tabaquin :)
"i" for Irish Joy Panimdim ;P
"me" for Meg Pelicano ^.^
